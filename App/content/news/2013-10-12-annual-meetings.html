2013 Annual Meetings session "Toward a Global Aid Map" October 12, 2013    

On October 12, 9:00–10:30 am EDT the Open Aid Partnership is hosting a session as a part of the Civil Society Program during the 2013 Annual Meetings of the International Monetary Fund (IMF) and World Bank Group (WBG). The session will demonstrate the power of open development data with a focus on the geography of aid and how open data can help to better engage with CSOs and citizens. The panel will be broadcasted online and include representatives from the governments of Bolivia and Kenya, and from ONE and Publish What You Fund.
